Cathedral and the Bazaar

I have wondered, for the past...oh I'd say at lest 15 years, where the internet came from. What is it? How does it work? Is it realy a series of tubes?

"Cathedral and the Bazaar" has been a peek behind the curtain at the Wizard himself. I am at once less confused and more in awe of the mysterious machine and the underground culture of hackers and pioneers that birthed it. After reading this book, I can honestly say that I know enough to hold a semi-intelligent conversation about the perks and perils of open-source programming.
